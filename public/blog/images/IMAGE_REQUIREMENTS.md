## Image Specifications

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Recommended Size:** 1200x800px (16:9 ratio) for hero images, 800x600px for content images
- **File Size:** Optimize for web (under 200KB per image)
- **Quality:** High quality but web-optimized
- **Background:** White or transparent for product images

## SEO Best Practices

All images should:
- Have descriptive, keyword-rich filenames
- Include proper alt text (already specified above)
- Be optimized for fast loading
- Include relevant keywords naturally in alt text
- Not be keyword-stuffed
