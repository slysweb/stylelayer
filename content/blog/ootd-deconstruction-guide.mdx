## What is Fashion Deconstruction?

Fashion deconstruction is the process of extracting individual items—like your **jacket, boots, and accessories**—from a portrait and rearranging them into a clean, minimalist layout. This style, often called *Knolling*, allows your audience to see every detail of your style at a glance.

### The Benefits for Creators:

* **Professional Aesthetic:** Instantly elevates your brand to a "magazine" level.
* **E-commerce Ready:** Perfect for selling pre-loved items on platforms like Depop or eBay.
* **Saves Hours:** No more tedious "Select and Mask" in Photoshop.

## 3 Tips for the Perfect AI Extraction

1. **Contrast is Key:** Wear clothes that contrast with your background for the cleanest edges.
2. **Mind the Posture:** Avoid crossing your arms over your chest; it makes it easier for the AI to "see" the full garment.
3. **Lighting Matters:** Soft, even lighting ensures that the fabric textures are captured realistically in the flat lay.
