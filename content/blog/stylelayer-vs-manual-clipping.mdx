# StyleLayer vs. Manual Clipping: How Much Time Can You Save?

If you're creating product photos for your e-commerce store, you're probably familiar with the tedious process of manually clipping backgrounds in Photoshop. Select, mask, refine edges, adjust—it's a time-consuming workflow that can take hours for a single product.

But what if there was a faster way? **StyleLayer AI** automates this entire process, but how much time does it actually save? Let's break down the numbers.

![Time comparison infographic: StyleLayer AI vs manual Photoshop clipping](/blog/images/time-comparison-hero.jpg "Visual comparison showing time savings between StyleLayer AI and manual clipping methods")

## The Manual Clipping Process: Step by Step

Let's start by understanding what manual clipping actually involves:

### Step 1: Open Image in Photoshop (30 seconds)

* Launch Photoshop
* Open your product photo
* Wait for file to load

### Step 2: Select the Product (5-15 minutes)

This is where it gets time-consuming:
* Use Quick Selection Tool or Pen Tool
* Manually trace around the product
* Adjust selection for complex edges
* Handle tricky areas (hair, fabric, transparent materials)

**Challenge:** Products with fine details, hair, or semi-transparent materials can take significantly longer.

### Step 3: Refine Edge (2-5 minutes)

* Open "Select and Mask" workspace
* Adjust edge detection
* Smooth and feather edges
* Remove background artifacts
* Fine-tune selection

### Step 4: Remove Background (1-2 minutes)

* Create layer mask
* Delete or hide background layer
* Check for missed areas
* Touch up any imperfections

### Step 5: Create White Background (1 minute)

* Create new white layer
* Position behind product
* Adjust if needed

### Step 6: Final Adjustments (2-5 minutes)

* Color correction
* Brightness/contrast adjustments
* Shadow removal
* Final touch-ups

**Total Time per Product: 12-30 minutes**

Products with:
* Very fine details (lace, mesh)
* Multiple transparent layers
* Complex lighting requirements

**However:** StyleLayer AI handles 95% of products perfectly, including most "complex" cases.

### 2. Artistic Creative Control

If you need:
* Custom backgrounds (not white)
* Artistic effects
* Specific styling requirements

**Note:** StyleLayer focuses on e-commerce-ready white backgrounds, which is what most sellers need.

### 3. Learning Photoshop

If you're:
* Learning design skills
* Building a portfolio
* Need Photoshop for other projects

**Reality:** Most sellers just want to sell products, not become designers.
